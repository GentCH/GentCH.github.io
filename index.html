About EventAIO

The application aims to provide a more convenient way for event organizer in managing event and deliver a better experience for participants during event check-in process. With the widely use of mobile, access to information about the events happening around is easy and fast. Participats do not need to worry about the attendance of the event as all check-in is in realtime. The application saves participants' from queuing and wait for thier turn to submit or fill in the check-in form manually. Check-in by scanning the QR code within a specific range of event location prevents participants in cheating attendance.
